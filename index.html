What type of caracters are the princesses?

Jasmine is a rather multi-layered character; incredibly independent and strong in many ways. She isn't afraid to speak her mind, no matter who she's up against.
Ariel is the 'spirit' servant for Prospero during his exile on a deserted island. Although Ariel is not human, he possesses many human characteristics, such as compassion and loyalty. He manipulates others when Prospero commands it.
Cinderella proves that imagination can be power, offering joy and Independence when outer world seems bleak, and training the mind to be resourceful.She uses her dreams and Imagination to cope from her stepmother and step sisters abuse.
